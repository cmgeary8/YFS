Program 04

Boot
camp

Idea to pitch in ~48 hours. Students work in parallel team roles — CEO, CFO, CMO — simulating a real business environment, not a classroom.

04
How The 48 Hours Flow
1

Plenary & Team Formation

Opening provocation, logistics, facilitator intros. Teams form, assign roles (CEO, CFO, CMO), agree a team name. First check-in mid-morning.

2

Ideation via SpeedRunning

Teams use the SpeedRunning tool to move from blank slate to validated idea and initial prototype. Output feeds directly into parallel workstreams.

3

Parallel Workstreams

Each role works concurrently — finance builds the model, marketing builds the GTM strategy, product develops the prototype. Real teamwork, not groupthink.

4

Check-ins & Milestones

Three structured check-ins tracked against rubric — attendance, progress, team collaboration. CEO is accountable for delivery.

5

Final Pitch

Teams present their full pitch to mentors and judges. Outputs: financial model, GTM strategy, prototype, and presentation deck.

💬 Sponsor Talking Point Bootcamp simulates a real work environment — not a classroom exercise. Students leave with real outputs: a business model, a financial plan, a prototype, and a pitch.
